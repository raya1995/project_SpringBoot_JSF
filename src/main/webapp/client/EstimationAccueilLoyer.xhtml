Why estimate the value of your home?


The realization of a real estate appraisal can have many motivations: a sale project following a professional transfer or the growing family or even the simple curiosity to know the value of his property. Before even asking a real estate agent for a home appraisal, it is possible and even recommended to make an appraisal online. Dari.tn is free, fast and without obligation


How to estimate a property for free?


The valuation of a property takes into account its location, its environment (shops, transport), its characteristics (condition, area, exposure ...) and demand. To refine the calculation of the sale price you can also use simulators or contact a local real estate agent.
